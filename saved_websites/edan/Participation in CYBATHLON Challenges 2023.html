                  Participation in CYBATHLON Challenges 2023

                  The nursing assistance robotics group Re-Enabling Robotics will participate as "TEAM EDAN" in the CYBATHLON of ETH Zurich on March 29, 2023.

                  The CYBATHLON is a non-profit project of ETH Zurich that challenges teams of developers around the world to develop assistance technologies suitable for everyday use together with and for people with physical disabilities. This time, among other things, there will be a new discipline: A race with assistance robots for people with severe impairment of arms and legs. Team EDAN will participate for the Institute of Robotics and Mechatronics in Oberpfaffenhofen. More information can be found here: EDAN | CYBATHLON ETH Zurich
